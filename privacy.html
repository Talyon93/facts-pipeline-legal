Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Overview

Facts Pipeline is a private content creation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when connecting to TikTok's API.

2. Data We Collect

The application does not collect personal data. It only stores a TikTok OAuth access token locally on the owner's device, used solely to publish videos to the owner's TikTok account.

3. How We Use Data

The OAuth token is used exclusively to upload videos to the authorized TikTok account. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

This application uses the TikTok Content Posting API, governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.
